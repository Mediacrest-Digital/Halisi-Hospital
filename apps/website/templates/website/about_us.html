Who We Are

Halisi Family Hospital is a private healthcare organization located in Kitengela, Kajiado County. 'Halisi' is a Swahili word that means being truthful, authentic, genuine and real. Our culture focuses on providing health and wellness services to families in a manner that reflects being 'Halisi'.
